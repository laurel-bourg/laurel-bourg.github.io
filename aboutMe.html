About Me

Hi! It's nice to meet you.

My name is Laurel Bourg (she/her). I am a physics undergraduate at Georgia Tech and an aspiring planetary scientists. My previous work includes modelling outburst flooding on ancient Mars and currently studying Venus crater melts. My experience involves planetary surface processes, but I also am interested in exoplanet research and using solar system planets as analogs for terrestrial worlds.
