About Me

Hi! It's nice to meet you.

My name is Laurel Bourg (she/her). I am a physics undergraduate at Georgia Tech and aspiring planetary scientists. I work with the PLANETAs and TECHtonics group to model outburst flooding on ancient Mars. My experience involves planetary surface processes, but I also am interested in exoplanet research and using solar system planets as analogs for terrestrial worlds.
